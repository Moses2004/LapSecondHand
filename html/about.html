About Us

Welcome to LAP Second Hand Phone Service! Our mission is to create a secure, trustworthy, and transparent marketplace for second-hand phones in Myanmar. In an era where new phones are increasingly expensive, we established this platform to help you easily buy high-quality pre-owned phones at affordable prices, or confidently sell your used devices.

We are committed to providing the best service to all our users. We build trust by transparently disclosing the condition of each phone and facilitating secure communication between buyers and sellers.

Why Choose LAP?

  • Reliability: Every phone is carefully inspected, and only genuine information is provided. We strive to protect users from fraudulent activities.
  • Ease of Use: Our buying and selling processes are designed to be simple and straightforward, ensuring a smooth experience for everyone.
  • Affordable Pricing: Access a wide range of quality pre-owned phones at competitive and fair prices.
  • Diverse Selection: Find a wide variety of popular brands and models, including iPhone, Samsung, Huawei, Oppo, and Xiaomi.
  • Customer Support: Our dedicated support team is always ready to assist you with any inquiries or issues you may encounter.
